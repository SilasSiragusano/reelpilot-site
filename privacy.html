Privacy Policy

BacktestLab · Last updated: 5 July 2026

BacktestLab ("the App") is a personal tool used by its individual owner to publish the owner's own short-form videos to the owner's own connected social media accounts. This policy explains what data the App handles.

Information we access

  • TikTok authorization tokens. When you connect your TikTok account, the App receives an access token via TikTok's official OAuth flow. It is used solely to upload videos to your own account through the TikTok Content Posting API.
  • Basic account identifiers (such as your open ID / username) needed to target the correct account for uploads.

The App does not collect, store, or process personal data belonging to other TikTok users, and does not access your private messages, contacts, or browsing data.

How we use it

Access tokens and identifiers are used only to publish the owner's own content to the owner's own account. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties.

Storage and retention

Tokens are stored locally on the owner's own machine/server and are kept only as long as needed to operate the App. They can be revoked at any time in your TikTok account settings ("Manage app permissions"), which immediately ends the App's access.

Data sharing

No user data is shared with third parties. The only external service the App communicates with is TikTok's official API, to publish the owner's own videos.

Your choices

You may disconnect the App and revoke its access at any time from your TikTok settings. For any request regarding your data, contact us at the address below.
