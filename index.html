How it works

1 · Backtest engine. Our research system simulates trading strategies on years of intraday market data — fixed position sizes, slippage included, every trade logged.
2 · Honest aggregation. Each run is stored alongside all other runs of the same strategy, so every video can state the full picture: how many symbols were tested and how many actually made money.
3 · Video rendering. The equity curve, stats and disclaimers are rendered into a 9:16 short with voiceover and captions — fully data-driven, one video per backtest run.
4 · Publishing. Finished videos are published to our own YouTube channel and TikTok account (@backtestlab59) through the platforms' official APIs. The integration posts to our own accounts only.

Principles

Show the losers. Most strategy videos online show one lucky chart. Every BacktestLab video includes the strategy's performance across all tested symbols — including the ones that lost money.

Drawdowns on screen. Maximum drawdown is displayed in every video, marked directly on the equity curve. If a strategy lost $18,000 on the way up, you'll see it.

Backtest ≠ promise. Every video carries a permanent on-screen and spoken disclaimer. We publish research, not trading signals, and we sell nothing.

FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. BacktestLab publishes backtested research results for educational and entertainment purposes only. Nothing here is a recommendation to buy or sell any security.

Where does the data come from?

From our own research system, which simulates strategies on historical intraday market data with fixed position sizing and slippage assumptions. Details are stated in each video.

Why are the win rates so low?

Trend-following strategies typically lose most trades and make up for it with a few large winners. That asymmetry — not a high win rate — is what the videos explore.

How does the TikTok/YouTube integration work?

BacktestLab uses the official TikTok Content Posting API and YouTube Data API to publish videos to our own accounts. There is no login area on this site and no third-party user data is accessed or collected.

Can I request a strategy?

Yes — comment under any video. The most requested strategies get backtested next.
